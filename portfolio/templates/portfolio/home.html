Building Plan Drawing

Building plans are graphical representations of how a structure will appear after completion. Builders and contractors utilize them to construct a variety of structures. Buidling Blueprints are also important for calculating the cost of a project and creating project budgets.

Architectural Design

Architectural design is a field that focuses on covering and meeting the needs and expectations of creating living environments via the use of certain tools and, most impotantly, creativity. As a result, despite widespread notion that architecture is solely a technological endeavor, the goal is to integrate the technological and the aesthetic.

3D Modelling

The process of constructing three-dimensional representations of an object or a surface is known as 3D modelling. 3D models are created using computer-based 3D modelling software. The size, form, and texture of an object can be determined throughout the 3D modelling process.

Structural Engineering Drawing

Structural engineering is a branch of civil engineering that is concerned with the design structural design of man-made structures. Often described as designing the 'bones and muscles' of structures, these engineers need to understand the rigidity, stability and strength of both buildings and non-building structures.

Bill of Quantities

A bill of quantities is a document used in the construction business to itemize materials, parts, and labor. It also specifies the terms and conditions of the construction or repair contract, as well as itemizes all work so that a contractor my price the work for which he or she is bidding.

Construction Servies

Construction services are the physical construction work performed by the CM/GC contractor to construct a project or part of a project (including early work packages). Construction services include all costs of performing, supervising and managing physical construction works.
